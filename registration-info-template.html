Before we get started, how did you get your charge point?
Employer Lease
I lease my EV and charge point through my employer
If this is your situation, please check both your work and personal email for an account activation message. Please use the link in this email to ensure your account is set up correctly. If you cannot find the activation email, please contact us.
Private Purchase / Lease
I purchased or privately lease my EV and charge point.
Please create an account to register your charge point and/or charge card. This situation applies to the following scenarios:
  • You purchased your charge point yourself
  • You received your charge point via private vehicle lease
  • You received your charge card along with your vehicle.
